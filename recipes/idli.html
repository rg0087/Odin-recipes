  • How to Make Soft Idli

    1. firstly, in a large bowl soak 1 cup urad dal for 2 hours
    2. also soak 2 cup of idli rice for hours.
    3. after1 hour of soaking of urad dal, drain off the water and transfer to grinder.
    4. grind for 45 minutes adding water as required. use dal soaked water foe grinding, as it helps in fermentation.
